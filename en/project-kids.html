Healthy Meals for School Breaks Program

VIEW WEBSITE

Description

In order to help disadvantaged children to eat well and have a good time during school breaks, the CWLF launched the " Healthy Meals for School Breaks Program ".The topic of the website is "fantasy of summer vacation". Children who have never experienced the summer vacation, can only dream of it and draw it on the paper. The illustrations make the site looks lively and happy. There are dynamic feedbacks during the donation process to make the donations more interesting.
